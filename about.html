Why we exist

A studio built
the way we
wished one was.

Before ApexWorks, our founder spent eight years on the client side, hiring agencies and freelancers for fast-growing brands. The pattern was always the same. Agencies were brilliant and slow, with a price tag to match. Freelance marketplaces were quick and cheap, but you gambled on quality every single time and managed the chaos yourself.

There was no middle. No one who could take a rough brief on Monday and hand back something genuinely good by Wednesday, at a price that made sense, without making you the project manager.

So we built it. ApexWorks is one point of contact backed by a network of specialists we have personally vetted. You tell us what you need. We match it to the right person, brief them properly, run the revisions, and deliver. You never see the scaffolding. You just get work that makes you look good.

"We are not trying to be the biggest studio in Los Angeles. We are trying to be the one you keep coming back to."

Theo Marchetti, Founder

What we refuse
to compromise on.

01

Taste is the whole job

Anyone can fill a brief. We are paid to have an opinion about what good looks like, and to push back when something can be better. Every deliverable goes out only if we would be happy to put our own name on it.

02

One contact, total clarity

You should never have to chase five freelancers or wonder who owns what. You get one person who knows your project cold and answers fast. We handle the sourcing, the briefing, and the herding behind the scenes.

03

Fast, without the panic

Speed comes from a tight process and the right specialist, not from rushing. Most projects land in 48 hours because the system is built for it, not because anyone is pulling an all-nighter.

04

We finish the job

Revisions are not a penalty or an upsell. They are how good work gets made. We do not close a project until you are genuinely happy with it, and that is written into how we price every engagement.

A short, fast year.

MAR 2026

Average turnaround settled at two days, and our first clients started sending their second and third briefs.
